What is this?

Winterpixelgames.com displays real-time data from Rocketbotroyale, Gooberdash, Goobershot, Gooberroyale, and Moonrock miners, and shows them in an accesible, easy-to-use interface. It is an ongoing project, and will be updated regularly. As of 5/27/2024, only the Gooberdash stats tab has been set up. Made by Tank8k.
